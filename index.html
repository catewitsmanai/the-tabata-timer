What is Tabata?

Tabata is a high-intensity interval training protocol developed by Dr. Izumi Tabata. The classic format: 20 seconds of maximum effort, 10 seconds of rest, repeated 8 rounds. Research shows it significantly improves both aerobic and anaerobic capacity in just 4 minutes.

How to use this timer

  • Pick a preset or tap the settings icon to set custom work, rest, and round values.
  • Press Start — or hit Spacebar — and follow the phase color: coral for work, sage for rest.
  • Sound cues fire in the final 3 seconds of each phase. Settings save automatically.

Use cases

  • Workouts — sprints, kettlebell circuits, bodyweight HIIT, mobility holds.
  • Studying — active recall sprints with short breaks to consolidate memory.
  • Focus sessions — deep work blocks with structured rest, lighter than Pomodoro.
  • Decluttering — timed effort bursts to stay in motion without burning out.
